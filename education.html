My Education

Growing up in Selangor, I attended Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Nirwana and finish my study until grade 6 and took Ujian Pencapaian Sekolah Rendah in that school. I also study at Sekolah Rendah Agama Jalan F, Kampung Pandan and took Ujian Penilaian Sekolah Rendah Agama there.





Once I entered secondary school, I started studying at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Pandan Jaya then got offered to further my study at Sekolah Menengah Agama Persekutuan Kajang in second half of 2017 until 2023. It was a good experience to live with my friends in boarding school and create so much beautiful memories together.





As SPM ended, I got offer letter from UiTM to study in Diploma in Library Informatics at UiTM Merbok, Kedah. The journey from my home to UiTM took me almost 6 hours in highway but it is also a nice experience as I'm learning to be more independance living far away from family.
